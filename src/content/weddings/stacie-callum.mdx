## How they met

Stacie and Callum met at a mutual friend's birthday in Melbourne — a night that neither of them planned on being particularly memorable. Seven years, one golden retriever, and three interstate moves later, they found themselves back in the Yarra Valley they'd grown to love together.

## The day

The morning began gently at the estate cottage — hair and makeup over coffee, bridesmaids drifting in and out, the soft hum of excitement you can only feel before something this meaningful. By early afternoon the light had turned that specific Yarra-Valley-gold that photographers dream about, and Stacie stepped into view for a first look that left Callum lost for words.

The ceremony unfolded under the old oak tree. Vows were spoken, mostly through happy tears. There was that moment — the one Callum's brother still brings up — when Stacie's Dad paused mid-speech, looked at her, and couldn't get the next line out. Nobody minded. We all just waited.

Portraits took us through the vineyard as the sun dropped, and the reception was everything a Yarra Valley celebration should be: long tables, Victorian wines, and a dance floor that genuinely didn't stop until last light.

## A story of the day

> "The part we didn't expect was how calm the day would feel. We kept being told it would fly by, and it did, but somehow we remember every moment."
> — Stacie
