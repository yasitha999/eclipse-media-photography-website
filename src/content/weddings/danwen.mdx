## How they met

Daniel and Wen met at a small Melbourne design studio where they both started on the same Monday. They ate lunch together for six months before either admitted there might be something there. Eventually, there was.

## The day

Their day spanned two cultures and two guest lists that had, until that morning, never met. Daniel's Mum cried during the tea ceremony, Wen's Dad cried during the English vows, and somehow nobody noticed when both families ended up dancing to the same song at 11pm.

Portraits took us through the city laneways Daniel and Wen had first walked together. The reception was warm, loud, full of speeches in two languages, and ended with a lion dance that nobody will ever forget.

> "It was everything we hoped for and a lot of things we didn't know to hope for."
> — Daniel & Wen
