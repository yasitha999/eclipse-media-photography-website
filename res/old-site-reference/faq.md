# FAQ

> Wedding photography FAQs from the original WordPress site.
> Reuse when splitting FAQ into its own `/faq` page (SEO-CHECKLIST §2).

---

**Can you hold my date?**
To be fair to all potential couples, I can pencil you in for 48 hours, but I cannot hold your date without a signed contract. A retainer and signed contract are required to officially reserve the wedding date.

**Are you insured?**
Yes. Most venues require liability insurance, and I can provide a certificate of proof if your venue requests one.

**What happens if an emergency/catastrophe arises and you can't be there?**
I have a large network of trusted photographers on backup should any major catastrophe arise. These vendors shoot in the same elegant and authentic style, and editing would still be handled by me.

**How soon will I receive my image gallery?**
You'll receive your online gallery link within 8 weeks of your wedding date (2 weeks for engagement sessions).

**When do we schedule our engagement session?**
Engagement sessions are only scheduled on weekdays, typically for sunrise or sunset — it keeps things intimate and avoids crowds.

**What if it rains?**
We set aside a backup plan or date for questionable weather, with rescheduling handled via email if needed.

**What do I wear?**
Something that makes you feel confident and beautiful! I'll send you a style guide with outfit suggestions.

**My fiancé and I hate having our picture taken — can you help?**
Absolutely. I'll coach you on posing with a natural approach that emphasises action, laughter and candid moments. Most couples tell me the camera disappears within the first 10 minutes.

**Do you travel for weddings?**
Yes — I love to travel. Travel fees are kept minimal and only cover actual expenses: flight, lodging, and a few meals.

**What happens if COVID (or similar) disrupts my original wedding date?**
The retainer fee is still non-refundable, but we'll work together as quickly as possible to find a solution that's amicable for both of us.

**How far in advance should I book?**
Most Melbourne couples book 10–16 months in advance, especially for popular Saturday dates in spring and autumn.

**How many photos will I receive?**
Between 400–800 fully edited, high-resolution images depending on your chosen package and the length of coverage.
