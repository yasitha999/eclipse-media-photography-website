# About Yasitha | Eclipse Media Photography

> Original About page copy from the WordPress site.

## Hero

**Hi, I'm Yasitha**

I'm Yasitha — a Melbourne wedding photographer with 10 years of experience and a genuine love for the people I work with.

Whether you're getting married in the Botanic Gardens, a city ballroom or a Yarra Valley winery — I'd love to be there.

## My approach

**I use unposed, authentic love, rather than staged perfection.**

Real emotion, not staged perfection. I use gentle guidance and organic movement so everything looks and feels natural. Your engagement session is a great way to ease into it — think of it as a relaxed practice run.

**I combine my experience with your unique story.**

I take time to get to know you before the day — what makes your relationship unique, what moments matter most. On the day itself, I'll keep things moving and capture everything while you just enjoy it.

From getting-ready moments to the last dance, I'm with you the whole way — there to help, guide, and document everything in between.

**Let's create images that reflect exactly who you are.**

## Background

I started with a camera in high school, fell into studio work after graduating, and photographed my first wedding a few years later. Ten years on, I'm still pinching myself that this is my job.

## A few minor (and fun) details about me

I love a good sunrise with coffee and friends. Real laughs, genuine love, fresh air, and the kind of dreams that bring you on a worldwide adventure.

- Shopping therapy on a rainy day is my absolute favourite thing.
- I love my sparkling wine and coffee.
- I'm not an avid reader — I prefer to be active, doing something with my hands — but I do enjoy a good podcast or audiobook.
- Planning adventures near and far fills up my free time.
- Korean dramas — need I say more?

## Closing

My style is candid, romantic and editorial — capturing genuine emotions and natural light to tell the story of your day exactly as it unfolds.
